City Guide / London Fashion Week

The Connaught, the shows, and the particular pleasure of Mayfair

By Isabelle Rowe February – March 2026 10 min read
← Back to The Edit

Carlos Place in February is quiet in the way only very expensive things are quiet. The Connaught sits at the end of it, a statement made so many times it no longer needs making.

London Fashion Week sits apart in the calendar. Not Paris with its symbolic weight, or Milan with its commercial gravity. London is where things are invented, then complicated by weather, distance, and a particular English restraint.

Hélène Darroze at The Connaught. Mount Street.

The Connaught sits at Carlos Place W1K 2AL. It knows what its guests need. Discretion. A bar worth returning to. A restaurant where dinner justifies the journey.

The Sutherland Suite. Carlos Place, Mayfair.

“

The Connaught Bar is the one place where you need not be anything in particular.

Isabelle Rowe

Three Michelin stars

Hélène Darroze at The Connaught is among London's finest dining rooms and entirely without pretense. The cooking celebrates seasonal produce from farmers and makers across France and the UK with a rigour that appears effortless. During fashion week it serves a single purpose: the cooking demands your full attention, which means you cannot think of anything else.

This is exactly what you need.

Aman Spa at The Connaught.

The Bar

Designed in 1926 and updated with perfect restraint, the bar is small and dark and immaculately proportioned. The cocktails are made by people who have thought seriously about them. During fashion week, the clientele spans an industry simultaneously exhausted and unable to sleep.

The bar releases you. The shows are finished. The front row is somewhere else. It does not care who you sat beside at any show. It makes your drink. A Martini. A Negroni. The house cocktail. Then leaves you alone with it.

The Stay

The Connaught is where you stay when you want the experience to matter. Everything is considered and the consideration is visible. The bar has thought about its cocktails. The restaurant about its cooking. The rooms about their proportions. The Aman Spa understands what an hour of silence in central London is worth.

Mayfair in February is cold, wet, magnificent. The Connaught is where you belong when it is all three.

The Splendid Edit visited The Connaught during London Fashion Week, February 2026. Rates from approximately £900 per night. Book through the-connaught.co.uk.
